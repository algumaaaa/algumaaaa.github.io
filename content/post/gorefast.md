## GORE THEM ALL.

Probably my favorite thing to come out of the miserable little event that was the pandemic.

I've always loved Doom, Blood, and Duke. That early 90s era of first person shooters now jokingly called "Boomer shooters", which I never quite understood. I feel I walk the fine line between being a millenial and a zoomer, but these sorts of games were still my childhood!

Gorefast is, if anything, a love letter to all of these FPS games. It originally started as just a way to pass time, evolved into what was going to be essentially left 4 dead but 90s aesthetic, feature-crept into something unwieldly, and got cut down into a neat little 4 level FPS game.

This is my first big game-dev project since I was a child. My teens to early adult life got carried away with quote unquote SERIOUS PROGRAMMING but that thankfully went away in favor of making bloody little games again.

This was an entirely solo project. Code, music, modelling, everything was just little old me. Which in part was great! It meant I had complete control of where everything went, and in part it was horrid! *I* had complete control of where everything went! Someday i'll write a post detailing the development of this game, as it is quite funny how stupid it all was.

If you like old style FPS games, give it a shot, its free.

Four levels, five weapons, hundreds of them.

Gore them all.

Gorefast is available at [itch](https://alguma.itch.io/gorefast)
