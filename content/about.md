## Hello!!

I'm Helena, my online handle is algumaaaa. Welcome to my page!

This started as just a place to keep all my projects organized in one place, but I decided to also post general blog stuff here too. I do a bit of everything so it's easy to get lost in all the output :p

### Programming

I'm a programmer who's worked mainly in game development for the past few years. Big fan of godot, shy disliker of Unreal. When i've some free time I like the gentle torture of coding small games in low level languages like C. 

Recently i've been branching out a bit, trying out some web development and all that. Its a bit too overwhelming for me with all the abstractions and stuff but i'm trying my best!

### Music

I'm a music major in the Universidade Estadual do Ceará. I play the piano and (mostly) focus on Bach. On the composing side of things I tend to lean towards electroacoustic music and its surroundings. Though I have dipped my toes into harsh noise, shoegaze, industrial, etc.
